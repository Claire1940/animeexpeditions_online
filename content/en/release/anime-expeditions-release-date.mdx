<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Progression: Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

Navigating the early stages of the game can be overwhelming due to the sheer volume of quests, banners, and upgrade paths. Following a structured progression route ensures you do not waste valuable gems or materials on low-tier units. Your primary objective during the first few hours is to complete the beginner quest path, which awards high-value summon tickets and guaranteed utility units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim Initial Group and Code Rewards">
    Before entering any combat stages, navigate to the top dropdown menu in the lobby to redeem active launch codes. Join the official developer group and like the game to claim a substantial package of starting gems and gold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Summon Your First 10 Units">
    Head to the summon area and perform a 10-pull on the standard banner. Do not worry if you fail to pull a top-tier legendary; this step is required to unlock the next phase of the beginner quest path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Conquer the School Grounds Map">
    Complete Acts 1 through 5 of the School Grounds (JJK-themed) map on Normal difficulty. Unlike other tower defense games that feature six acts per map, this game streamlines progression with only five acts per zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip and Feed Your Core Units">
    Equip any runes or weapons (such as the Kunai) dropped during your story runs onto your strongest unit. Navigate to your inventory and feed low-tier food units to your primary damage dealer to increase their base level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Completing these initial steps will finalize Chapter 1 of the beginner path, rewarding you with 25 beginner tickets and 25 evolution essences. Repeat this process for Chapter 2 to reach the 50-summon pity threshold on the beginner banner, guaranteeing your first Mythic unit to carry you through mid-game content.

| Step | Objective | Key Reward | Practical Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Step 1** | Redeem Codes & Group Rewards | Starting Gems & Gold | Funds your initial 10-pull summon |
| **Step 2** | Summon 10 Units | Random Roster Additions | Unlocks Chapter 1 progression |
| **Step 3** | Complete School Grounds Acts 1-5 | Kunai Rune & XP Food | Boosts unit damage and level caps |
| **Step 4** | Feed & Equip Units | Stat Boosts | Prepares roster for Hard Mode challenges |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Mechanics: Ghost Placement & Evolution">

To transition from a casual player to an expert, you must master the game's unique quality-of-life mechanics and progression loops. The most notable of these is the ghost pre-placement system. This feature allows you to place a translucent "ghost" version of a unit on the map even if you lack the required funds. The moment your active cash matches the unit's cost, the system deploys them automatically.

Once you secure a powerful Mythic unit like Yuta, your immediate focus should shift toward evolution. Evolving a unit dramatically increases their base stats, unlocks new passive abilities, and alters their visual appearance.
