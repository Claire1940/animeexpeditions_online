Once you have secured a powerful Mythic unit, the next step in optimization is the Trait system. Traits are permanent passive bonuses that can be rerolled at the Upgrade station. During early access, Trait Crystals are somewhat limited, so knowing when to stop rerolling is vital for resource management.

The "Unbound" trait is currently the most sought-after, offering a massive 350% damage boost, but it limits you to a single placement of that unit. For most players, "Primordial" or "Forsaken" are more versatile options.

| Trait | Roll Rate | Main Bonus | Best For |

<Callout type="warning" title="Reroll Warning">
Do not chase "Unbound" on support units. Save your Trait Crystals for your primary damage dealers. A support unit with a simple "Range" or "Speed" trait is often more than sufficient.
</Callout>

Evolution is the final stage of unit progression. By collecting specific materials from Story stages, Expeditions, and Raids, you can transform your units into their "Divine" or "Awakened" forms. This process usually requires the unit to be at a specific level and costs a significant amount of Yen. Evolution not only boosts base stats but often adds new mechanics, such as increased range or additional ability procs.
