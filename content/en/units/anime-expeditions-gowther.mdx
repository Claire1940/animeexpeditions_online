<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Strategy: King's Tomb Mastery">

Using **anime expeditions gowther** effectively requires more than just placing him and walking away. His placement and the timing of his Rewind ability are the difference between a successful clear and a defeat. In the King's Tomb mastery stage, positioning him at the first major "U-turn" allows his AoE to hit enemies twice.

1.  **Early Game**: Start with one Gowther and focus on economy. His base 900 damage is enough to solo the first 5-8 waves.
2.  **Mid Game**: Max out your farms before going for the final Gowther upgrades. You need the cash flow to reach his 14,000 damage threshold.
3.  **Late Game**: Watch the boss's movement. As soon as the boss enters the center of your kill zone, ensure the **Puppet Mark** is applied. If the boss moves too fast, trigger the **Rewind** ability immediately.

<Callout type="warning" title="Placement Warning">
Do not place your two Gowthers too close together. Spacing them out ensures that the Puppet Mark is consistently applied to new waves of enemies rather than overlapping on the same target.
</Callout>
