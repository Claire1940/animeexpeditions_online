<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Traits and Rerolls for Elf Mage">

Optimizing your unit requires visiting the Trait station located in the Upgrades hub. Rolling passive traits permanently alters the unit's combat behavior, allowing you to tailor its performance to specific roles. For a magic-focused hybrid like this one, reducing the Seconds Per Attack (SPA) while expanding the attack range is just as important as boosting raw damage.

While the elusive Unbound trait offers the highest theoretical damage increase, it restricts your deployment to a single placement. The Primordial trait stands out as the absolute best-in-slot option, providing a balanced blend of offensive power and utility without any placement limitations.
