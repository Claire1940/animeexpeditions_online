<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unit Ascension and Evolution Guide">

To reach the maximum potential of your **anime expeditions units**, you must utilize the Ascension system. Unlike simple leveling, Ascension requires duplicate units and specific Sprites to unlock higher stat tiers. For Mythic units, reaching Max Ascension involves sacrificing multiple copies, which emphasizes the need for efficient gem farming.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Unit">
    Secure your target Mythic or Secret unit through summoning or raids.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Sprites">
    Farm the required elemental Sprites from daily challenge stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Merge Duplicates">
    Use the Ascension menu to combine duplicates. Note: You typically need 3 units for a full ascension cycle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evolve to Final Form">
    Craft specific evolution items (like the Shinagami Sword for Kachi) to unlock the unit's true form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Ascension Level | Requirement | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | 1 Duplicate + Sprites | +15% Base Damage |
| **Tier 2** | 1 Duplicate + Sprites | +25% Damage, +10% Range |
| **Max Tier** | 1 Duplicate + Rare Sprites | Unlocks Special Passive scaling |

<Callout type="tip" title="Evolution Tip">
Always lock your main units before performing bulk Ascensions. It is very easy to accidentally sacrifice a high-stat unit if you are not paying attention to the selection screen.
</Callout>
