<Section icon={Shield} title="Passive Abilities and Skill Set">

The true power of **anime expeditions kenpachi** lies in his complex passive abilities. Unlike standard units that simply deal damage, Kenpachi interacts with the battlefield in multiple ways, including self-cleansing stuns and executing low-health enemies.

**Key Passives Breakdown:**
- **Dynamic Damage:** Increases damage by approximately 0.56% for every enemy currently within his range (capped at 15 enemies).
- **Aura Burn:** Enemies within the inner 40% of his range take 5% of Kenpachi's current damage every second.
- **Stun Cleanse:** If Kenpachi is stunned, he immediately removes the effect and performs a powerful counter-attack.
- **Executioner:** When an enemy falls below 10% health, Kenpachi performs a follow-up attack at 100% damage to finish them off.
- **Berserk State:** After 25 successful takedowns, he enters a 15-second frenzy, boosting damage and decreasing SPA.
