<Section icon={Map} title="Story Mode and Farming Strategy">

Progression in Anime Expeditions is gated by Story Mode. Each map, such as the initial "School Grounds," consists of five acts. Clearing these on Hard difficulty is mandatory for serious players, as it provides double the experience and currency compared to Normal mode. With the **anime expeditions true saint**, Hard mode becomes significantly more manageable.

**The "Economy First" Strategy:**
1. **Initial Placement**: Start the match by placing your Stone Alchemist or Roman.
2. **Upgrade Farms**: Do not place damage units immediately. Let the enemies walk a bit while you funnel all cash into upgrading your farm units.
3. **Safety Net**: Place a single True Saint near the end of the path or at a strategic choke point once the first few enemies get close to your base.
4. **Auto-Upgrade**: Once your farms are maxed, turn on the auto-upgrade feature for your damage units.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| School Grounds | Hard | 2x Gems/Exp | Level 1-10 |
| Frozen Peaks | Hard | 2x Gems/Exp | Level 10-20 |
| Desert Ruins | Hard | 2x Gems/Exp | Level 20-30 |
| Mystic Woods | Hard | 2x Gems/Exp | Level 30+ |
