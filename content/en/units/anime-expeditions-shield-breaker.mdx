Evolving your characters is the single most effective way to unlock their true potential and maximize your shield-breaking capabilities. <Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Story Mode on Normal">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Sprite Stones from Challenges">
    Participate in Daily and Regular Challenges to accumulate basic gray Sprite Stones. Avoid the Weekly Challenges early on, as they are tuned for high-level endgame accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft Colored Sprites">
    Visit the crafting station in the main lobby. Convert your basic gray sprites into the required Pink and Purple Sprites. The exchange rate is **5 gray sprites** for every **1 colored sprite**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Puppet Unit Trial">
    Enter the specialized Unit Trial under the trial menu. You will be given a preset team to clear 15 waves. Successfully defeating the boss rewards you with the essential **Puppet Heart** evolution item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Evolve and Reroll Stats">
    Navigate to the upgrade menu, select your Puppet unit, and combine the Puppet Heart with your crafted sprites. Once evolved, use your stat reroll tokens to secure high-tier damage and speed modifiers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Traits and Stat Rerolls for Shield Breakers">

Traits play a massive role in defining a unit's combat efficiency. A shield-breaking unit with a poorly optimized trait will struggle to keep debuffs active on fast-moving targets. When spending your hard-earned reroll tokens, aim for traits that boost damage, attack speed, or range.

The absolute best trait for a support-oriented shield breaker is **Unbound**, which drastically reduces skill cooldowns and increases attack speed, allowing for near-permanent uptime on debuff marks. However, if you roll **Primordial**, keep it, as it offers a massive boost to raw damage and range.
