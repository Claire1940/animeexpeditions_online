<Section icon={Swords} title="Trial Mechanics and Boss Phases">

The boss of the **anime expeditions elf mage trial** is a mirror version of the Elf Mage herself, possessing massive AOE (Area of Effect) capabilities. Understanding the phase transitions is vital to surviving the ten-minute encounter.

| Phase | Health Threshold | Key Mechanic | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | 100% - 75% | Mana Siphon | Place units on the outer edges |
| **Phase 2** | 74% - 40% | Mirror Image | Identify and kill the clone with a shadow |
| **Phase 3** | 39% - 10% | Meteor Storm | Rotate defensive cooldowns |
| **Enrage** | Below 10% | Infinite Mana | All-out burst damage |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Placement">
    Position your healers at the bottom-left corner. The boss starts with a central AOE that hits the middle 9 squares of the grid immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Clone Hunt">
    During Phase 2, three clones appear. Only the clone with the purple aura takes full damage. Wasting ultimates on the wrong clone will lead to a timeout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Shield Breaking">
    When the boss reaches 25% HP, she generates a 1-million HP shield. Use multi-hit units rather than single-hit nukers to deplete the shield layers quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Success">
Using units with "Silence" or "Stun" can interrupt the Meteor Storm in Phase 3, giving your team a 5-second window of safety to heal up.
</Callout>
