<Section icon={Target} title="Kenpachi Raid: Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

To secure **anime expeditions secret units**, you must master the specific raids associated with them. The Kenpachi Raid is notoriously difficult due to the boss's passive ability to steal 10% HP from any enemy that isn't killed instantly. This requires a high-damage setup and often necessitates playing with a coordinated team.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Purchase Raid Totems">
    Before entering, spend 75 Spirit Tokens to buy Raid Totems. These provide a massive damage multiplier (up to 1.9x) which is mandatory for soloing or fast clears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Team Composition">
    Bring support units like Rukia to slow enemies. Even with a secret unit, the slow mechanics are vital for preventing the boss from reaching the 10% HP steal threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Placement">
    Place your primary damage dealers toward the back of the map initially. The stages are long, and the "Unbound" mechanics work best when enemies are clustered in the mid-lane.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Boss Execution">
    When the Kenpachi boss appears, focus all upgrades on your strongest AOE unit. Use the "Berserk" state triggered by takedowns to burst the boss down before he can heal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Token Farming">
You earn **15 tokens per stage** clear. Since totems cost 75, you should aim to farm lower-difficulty raids to bank tokens before attempting the Secret Unit grind.
</Callout>

| **Stun Waves** | Units are disabled for 3s | Equipment with "Stun Cleanse" |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Secret Unit Passives and Mechanics">

Understanding the hidden passives of **anime expeditions secret units** is key to maximizing their DPS. Kenpachi, for instance, has a "Berserk" state that triggers after 25 takedowns. During this 15-second window, his damage skyrockets while his SP cost decreases, allowing for a devastating flurry of attacks.

Another critical mechanic is the "Execute" passive. When an enemy falls below 10% health within Kenpachi's range, he performs an immediate follow-up attack at 100% damage, effectively finishing off weakened bosses or elites.
