<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Evolve Ichigo">

Evolving Ichigo is essential to unlocking his true potential, transforming him from a decent Legendary unit into a top-tier Mythic damage dealer. The evolution process grants him one new attack, one new active ability, two powerful passive skills, and three additional upgrade tiers. To initiate the evolution, players must gather specific crafting materials and complete a specialized Unit Trial.

| Material Type | Quantity Required | Primary Acquisition Source | Crafting Cost (Gold) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Yellow Sprites** | 10 | Daily Challenges / Base Crafting | 500 |
| **Red Sprites** | 5 | Sprite Challenge / Advanced Crafting | 1,500 |
| **Rainbow Sprites** | 1 | Weekly Raids / Elite Crafting | 5,000 |
| **Vizard Mask** | 1 | Ichigo Unit Trial (Primordial Mode) | None (Quest Reward) |

To obtain the Vizard Mask, players must enter the Unit Trial matchmaking lobby. During this trial, Ichigo is scaled with primordial attributes, making him incredibly strong. The placement cost for Ichigo during the trial is 700 Gold. His unique relocation ability allows players to move him anywhere on the map; upon relocation, he leaves behind a physical clone at his previous location that continues to deal **50% of his total damage**.
