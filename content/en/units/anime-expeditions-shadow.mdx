<Section icon={Map} title="Combat Strategy and Unit Synergy">

Using the **anime expeditions shadow** unit effectively requires more than just placing him on the map. Because his upgrades are expensive and his summons require strategic placement, you need a supporting cast to handle early-wave pressure.

**Placement Strategy:**
1. **Early Game**: Place a farm unit (like Saint Queue) immediately to build your economy.
2. **Mid Game**: Place the Shadow unit in a central location that covers multiple path bends. This maximizes the Owl's circling area.
3. **Shield Management**: Use units like **True Saint** (Day 3 login reward) to strip enemy shields. The Shadow unit deals massive raw damage but can struggle against high-shield counts in daily challenges.
4. **Ability Timing**: Save the Shadow Realm for boss waves or when enemies are nearing the exit. The rewind mechanic is a literal life-saver.

| Synergistic Unit | Role | Synergy Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **True Saint** | Shield Breaker | Clears path for Shadow's high damage |
| **Saint Queue** | Economy / Farm | Funds the high cost of Mahoraga |
| **Hollow** | Secondary DPS | Benefits from Shadow Realm magic buff |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Tip">
Don't rush the Mahoraga upgrade if you are struggling with fast, weak enemies. The 15-second stun on the main unit can lead to a leak if you don't have other towers covering the lane.
