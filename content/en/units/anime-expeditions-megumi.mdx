<Section icon={Swords} title="Upgrade Path & Summon Mechanics">

In battle, Megumi functions as a hybrid summoner and domain-control unit. His upgrade path is expensive, requiring a robust economy, but each milestone unlocks a completely new mechanic or summoned beast that fights alongside him on the battlefield.

| Upgrade Level | Cost (Yen) | Attack Type | Unlocked Summon / Passive | Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base** | 1,500 | Single Target | None | Moderate physical damage |
| **Upgrade 3** | 4,500 | Splash AOE | **Nue (Wing Spirit)** | Deals 75% damage, applies stuns |
| **Upgrade 5** | 8,000 | Splash AOE | **Divine Dog (Wolf Spirit)** | High-frequency physical attacks |
| **Upgrade 6** | 12,000 | Cone AOE | **Bunny Swarm** | Changes basic attack to wide cone |
| **Upgrade 8** | 18,000 | Cone AOE | **Shadow Realm** | Domain expansion, teleports enemies |
| **Upgrade 10** | 35,000 | Full Map | **Mahoraga (Divine Spirit)** | CC immune, scaling execution damage |

Understanding how these summons interact is key to clearing high-difficulty content. Nue circles at 50% of Megumi's total attack range, dealing consistent damage and keeping fast-moving enemies CC-locked. The Divine Dog acts as a secondary single-target DPS unit, shredding high-HP targets that slip past your front line. 

At Upgrade 8, Megumi unlocks his active ability: Chimera Shadow Garden (Shadow Realm). When activated, the entire map transforms, triggering a "Shadow Rewind" that teleports all active enemies back toward their spawn point by 50% of Megumi's range. This ability can be used twice per match and applies a 25% damage buff to all allied magical units for 30 seconds.

Finally, reaching Upgrade 10 summons Mahoraga. While Megumi is self-stunned for 15 seconds during the summoning process, Mahoraga is completely unstoppable. He cannot be stunned, frozen, or killed. He leaps across the map, targeting the strongest enemies, and gains a permanent 1% damage boost per kill, capping at a 100% total damage increase.

<Callout type="success" title="Summon Scaling">
If Megumi possesses the Unbound trait, every single one of his summons (Nue, Divine Dog, and Mahoraga) inherits the Unbound status, multiplying their base attack speed and range.
</Callout>

To push Megumi to his absolute limits, you must pair him with top-tier traits and complementary support units. Traits modify a unit's base stats, attack speed, and range, drastically changing their viability in late-game challenges.

Megumi's high upgrade costs mean he cannot function effectively without a strong economic backbone. Units like Ramen Guy or Sentru equipped with the Investor trait are mandatory additions to your team. They generate the massive amounts of Yen required to rush Megumi to Upgrade 8 and 10 early in the match.

Additionally, support units that apply damage-amplifying debuffs work exceptionally well with Mahoraga's execution scaling. Gowther is a prime example; his puppet mark inflicts a 20% damage-taken debuff on the strongest enemy within his range. When placed near Megumi's domain, Gowther ensures Mahoraga quickly executes bosses and elite enemies, rapidly stacking his passive damage buff.
