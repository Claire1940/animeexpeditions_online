<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Anime Expeditions Equipment Reroll System">

Once the Armory is open, you can begin utilizing the **anime expeditions equipment reroll** system to maximize the efficiency of your units. Every piece of equipment drops with a randomized percentage range for its primary and secondary attributes. Rerolling allows you to reset these numbers in search of a perfect stat roll.

To execute a reroll, you must acquire Equipment Reroll Scrolls. These are primarily purchased from the in-game Gold Shop for 2,500 Gold each, though they also drop as rewards from high-tier challenges. Because stat ranges can vary significantly, achieving a perfect roll often requires multiple attempts.

To protect your progress, the game features an Equipment Lock mechanic. If you land a maximum roll on a highly desirable stat, such as a 10% Damage increase, you can purchase and apply an Equipment Lock. This prevents that specific stat from changing while you continue to reroll the remaining attributes on the item.

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Management">
Always save your Equipment Locks for exclusive or unit-specific gear. Standard equipment is easily replaced, making locks too valuable to waste on early-game items.
</Callout>

| Reroll Item | Gold Cost | Source | Main Utility |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Reroll Scroll** | 2500 Gold | Gold Shop / Challenges | Resets all unlocked stat percentages |
| **Equipment Lock** | 5000 Gold | Gold Shop / Events | Freezes a specific stat during rerolls |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Crafting and Forging Equipment">

The Forge menu inside the Level 2 Armory allows you to craft both standard and unit-specific equipment. Crafting is the most reliable way to obtain high-tier gear without relying solely on low-percentage drops from Story Mode stages.

Unit-specific equipment offers massive stat boosts and unlocks game-changing passive abilities. However, these items can only be equipped by their designated characters, and most require the character to be fully evolved first. Standard equipment, while possessing lower stat ceilings and lacking unique passives, can be shared freely among any unit in your roster.

To craft these items, you must collect Equipment Scraps and elemental Orbs by completing maps in Expedition Mode. Gathering these materials consistently is the key to gearing up your entire roster.

<Section icon={Target} title="Expedition Mode Farming and Resource Routes">

To fund your crafting and rerolling endeavors, you must master Expedition Mode. This mode is the primary source of Cursed Timber, Gold, and upgrade materials needed to maintain your Armory.

When starting an Expedition, open the map interface directly below the stage information. This allows you to plan your route. To maximize your Armory progression, always select paths that offer increased Cursed Timber drops. 

During the defense phases, place your farm units near the outer edges of the moving payload to maximize their utility without blocking your main defenders. Position your heavy DPS units near the center of the payload to ensure maximum coverage against incoming waves. Set your farm units to auto-upgrade priority level one to optimize your income generation early in the run.
