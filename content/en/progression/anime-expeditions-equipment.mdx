<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Obtain & Craft Equipment">

Securing the best **anime expeditions equipment** requires a mix of luck, persistence, and resource management. The game employs a "Mastery" system for story maps, where higher difficulty levels yield better rewards. If RNG (Random Number Generation) is not on your side, the Armory Forge serves as a deterministic path to power.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Map Mastery">
    Complete the base story mode for a region (e.g., Spirit City or Rose Kingdom) to unlock Mastery difficulty. This is the only place where exclusive gear drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grind the Pity Meter">
    Each Mastery run has a 5% chance for the exclusive item. If you fail 19 times, the 20th run is a guaranteed drop. Monitor your run count in the stage selection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize the Armory Forge">
    Visit the Expedition Hub to craft specific items like the Hex Blade or Technique Amplifier. This usually requires materials gathered from Raids and high-level challenges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip and Evolve">
    Open your unit inventory, select an evolved character, and slot the gear. Remember that a unit can only hold one piece of equipment at a time in the early game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Rerolling and Optimizing Stats">

Getting the item is only half the battle. To truly optimize your **anime expeditions equipment**, you must engage with the reroll system. Every piece of gear drops with a range of potential stats (e.g., +1% to +10% Damage). If your legendary Promise Ring drops with minimum stats, its effectiveness is severely hampered.

| Reroll Item | Source | Cost | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Reroll** | Gold Shop / Challenges | 2,500 Gold | Randomizes all stats on one item |
| **Equipment Lock** | Premium Shop / Rare Drops | Variable | Locks one stat while rerolling others |
| **Stat Reset** | Special Events | N/A | Resets item to base for a fresh start |

The goal for any serious player is to hit the "Perfect" roll. For damage-oriented units, this means +10% Damage and -5% SPA. For support units, focus on +10% Range to ensure their buffs or debuffs cover the widest possible area of the map.

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Advice">
Only use Equipment Locks on gear that already has one "Perfect" stat. For example, if you have -5% SPA but only +2% Damage, lock the SPA before rerolling to find that +10% Damage.
</Callout>
