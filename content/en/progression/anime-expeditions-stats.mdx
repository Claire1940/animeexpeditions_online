<Section icon={Target} title="Stat Rerolls and Potential System">

The core of the **anime expeditions stats** system lies in the Stat Potential menu. When you select a unit in the upgrade section, you will see a list of their current potential ratings. These ratings are not permanent; you can use Stat Rerolls to gamble for better percentages. This is particularly important for Mythical units that you plan to use in high-level Raids or Expeditions.

| Item Type | Primary Use | Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stat Reroll** | Changes all unlocked stats on a unit | Gold Shop, Codes, Raid Rewards |
| **Stat Lock** | Prevents a specific stat from being changed | Gold Shop, Special Events |
| **Trait Reroll** | Changes the unit's Enchant/Trait | Crafting, Music Shop, Raids |
| **Shiny Transfer** | Moves shiny status to a normal unit | Upgrade Section (Shiny Transfer) |

When rerolling, the game generates new values for every slot that isn't locked. If you land an "S" tier attack speed stat, you should immediately use a Stat Lock before rolling again. Keep in mind that using locks increases the cost of subsequent rerolls, so prioritize locking only the most impactful stats for that specific unit type.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Stat Locks are rarer than Rerolls. Only use them on Mythical or Secret units. Using them on Rare or Epic units is generally considered a waste of resources in the early game.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Stat Transfer Mechanics">

One of the most player-friendly features regarding **anime expeditions stats** is the ability to transfer stats between units. If you have spent dozens of rerolls getting a legendary unit to perfect stats, but then you summon a more powerful Mythical version of that same character, you don't have to start over. The Stat Transfer system allows you to migrate those high-tier attributes directly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Upgrade Menu">
    Navigate to the Upgrade section in the main lobby and select the 'Stat Transfer' option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select the Source Unit">
    Choose the unit that currently possesses the high-tier stats you want to move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select the Target Unit">
    Choose the new, more powerful unit that will receive the stats. Note that the source unit's original stats will be overwritten.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm the Transfer">
    Pay the required gold or material cost to finalize the process. Your new unit now has the optimized stat spread.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Transfer Type | Cost Level | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal to Normal** | Low | Stats move, source unit remains |
| **Shiny to Normal** | Medium | Target becomes Shiny, source is consumed |
| **Mythic Transfer** | High | Full stat migration between high-tier units |

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Note">
This mechanic makes it safe to invest in your current best units. You never "lose" progress because those stats can always be moved to the next meta-defining character you pull.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Traits and Critical Optimization">

Beyond the standard **anime expeditions stats**, Traits (also known as enchants) provide a massive layer of power. Traits are separate from the potential system and offer unique multipliers that can define a unit's role in your team. For example, a unit with high base range but low speed can be "fixed" with a speed-oriented trait.

The "Precision" trait is currently one of the most sought-after enchants in the 2026 meta. Precision II, for instance, offers a 10% increase to Critical Damage and a 20% increase to Critical Chance. When combined with S-tier base critical stats, your units can achieve nearly 100% crit uptime, effectively doubling their DPS in Raids.

| Trait Name | Stat Bonus | Rarity/Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Precision I** | +5% Crit Dmg, +10% Crit Chance | Common (15%) |
| **Precision II** | +10% Crit Dmg, +20% Crit Chance | Rare (4%) |
| **Swiftness** | +15% Attack Speed | Uncommon (10%) |
| **Giant** | +20% Range, +10% Damage | Rare (2%) |
| **Godly** | +50% Damage, +20% Speed | Secret (&lt;1%) |

<Callout type="info" title="Trait Index">
You can view the full list of potential enchants in the Trait Index. This helps you decide whether to keep a current roll or risk it for a better multiplier.
</Callout>

To truly maximize your **anime expeditions stats**, you must look into Evolution and Ascension. Ascension requires you to have multiple copies of the same Mythical unit. By sacrificing three duplicates, you can ascend your primary unit, which grants a massive multiplier to all existing base stats.

| Evolution Stage | Requirements | Stat Multiplier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Unit** | None | 1.0x |
| **Ascended I** | 3 Duplicate Units | 1.5x |
| **Evolved** | Specific Crafting Materials | 2.0x + New Ability |

Equipment also plays a role. At the end of matches, equipment can drop randomly. These items can be equipped to provide flat bonuses to your unit's stats, such as +100 Attack or +5% Range. Like units, equipment can be rerolled and locked to ensure you are getting the best possible bonuses for your loadout.
