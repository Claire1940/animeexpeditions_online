<Section icon={Target} title="Optimizing Stats and Potential">

Beyond traits, **anime expeditions shiny units** can be further optimized through the Stat Potential system. This allows you to reroll individual stats like Damage, Range, and SBA to reach the coveted **Z-rank** or **SS-rank**. This process requires Stat Reroll tokens, which are primarily obtained through raids and special events.

The stat reroll UI allows you to "lock" specific stats that you want to keep. If you roll an SS-rank in Damage but a C-rank in Range, you can lock the Damage stat and continue rerolling the others. This is the final step in creating a "Perfect" unit.

| Stat Rank | Multiplier Range | Color Indicator |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Z Rank** | **Max Potential** | Rainbow / Gold |
| **SS Rank** | High Potential | Red / Orange |
| **S Rank** | Great Potential | Purple |
| **A-B Rank** | Average | Blue / Green |

<Callout type="info" title="Stat Locking">
Locking a stat increases the cost of the next reroll. Only lock stats that are at least S-rank or higher to ensure you aren't wasting tokens on mediocre rolls.
</Callout>

</Section>
