<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm Trait Crystals and Rerolls">

Acquiring the currency for **anime expeditions traits** requires a multi-faceted approach. Because there is no unlimited "grindable" source like in some other games, you must maximize every available daily, weekly, and one-time reward.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Story Challenges">
    Each story stage offers three specific challenges across Normal and Hard difficulties. Completing these provides 3 rerolls per stage. Across 5 worlds with 6 stages each, you can accumulate approximately 155 rerolls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Participate in Raids">
    Clearing raids rewards players with Raid Tokens and Spirit Tokens. These can be exchanged in the Raid Shop and Spirit City Shop for a weekly stock of Trait Crystals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Daily and Weekly Quests">
    Consistent play is rewarded. Daily quests provide about 5 rerolls, while weekly quests offer 15. The Weekly Challenge is particularly lucrative, granting 50 Trait Crystals upon completion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Event Participation">
    Check the "Events" tab frequently. The Beginner's Path and Creator Spotlight events offer substantial amounts of event coins, which can be traded for dozens of rerolls in the event shop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Beyond these methods, the Expedition game mode features a resource drill. By upgrading this drill, you can obtain Geodes, which have a chance to drop Trait Crystals among other valuable resources. While the drop rate is lower, it provides a passive income stream for long-term players.

| Source Type | Frequency | Estimated Yield |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Story Challenges | One-time | ~155 Total Rerolls |
| Weekly Challenge | Weekly | 50 Trait Crystals |
| Raid Shop | Weekly | Varies by performance |
| Daily Quests | Daily | 5 Trait Crystals |
| Level Milestones | One-time | Small incremental rewards |

<Callout type="warning" title="Pity System Notice">
The game tracks your rerolls. High-tier traits like Unbound have a pity threshold (approximately 10%). Ensure you have enough crystals saved up to reach this threshold if you are targeting the absolute best traits.
</Callout>
