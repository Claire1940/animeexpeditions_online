<Section icon={Swords} title="Trait Rerolls & Stat Potential Systems">

Once you secure your Mythic units, maximizing their combat potential requires engaging with the Trait Rerolling and Stat Potential systems. Traits grant unique passive modifiers that can completely alter a unit's role, while Stat Potentials provide raw multiplier bonuses to damage, range, and attack speed.

The Stat Potential system features a player-friendly Worthiness Bar. When rolling for stats, any excess energy generated by the roll overfills the bar and is saved for subsequent rolls, rather than being wasted. Stats are ranked from standard tiers up to S, SS, and Z (the game's equivalent of a Godly roll).

| Trait Name | Placement Limit | Core Combat Effects | Meta Viability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unique / Monarch** | 1 Unit per map | Massive damage boost, expanded attack range | Tier 1 (Best for main DPS) |
| **Primordial** | No placement limit | High baseline damage and speed multipliers | Tier 1 (Excellent generalist) |
| **Crit Trait** | No placement limit | Elevated critical strike chance and crit damage | Tier 2 (Strong for fast attackers) |
| **DOT & Cost Trait** | No placement limit | Applies damage-over-time, reduces placement cost | Tier 2 (Excellent for utility units) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Trait reroll items are scarce. Avoid using your rerolls on Rare or Legendary units. Save your resources exclusively for top-tier Mythic units to ensure you do not hit a progression wall in late-game content.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Progression, Unit Trials, and End-Game Loops">

Anime Expeditions introduces several quality-of-life improvements that streamline the traditional tower defense grind. The most notable is the Unit Trials system. Instead of grinding difficult legend stages for dozens of hours to evolve a character, players can enter a Unit Trial. This mode provides a preset team and challenges you to beat a specific stage; succeeding awards the unit's evolution item immediately.

Upon evolving a unit, you unlock their third equipment slot. This slot is dedicated to character-specific unique equipment, which functions as a second passive ability. This equipment can be farmed from the Mastery stages of the game's five main maps.
