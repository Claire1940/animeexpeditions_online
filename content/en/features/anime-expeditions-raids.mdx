<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm the Kenpachi Raid Secret Unit">

Farming the secret Kenpachi unit in **anime expeditions raids** is a test of endurance and resource management. The boss in this raid is particularly notorious for its passive ability: it steals 10% HP from all nearby enemies. If you do not clear the smaller mobs quickly, the boss will effectively have infinite health, making the stage impossible to finish within the time limit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Currency Accumulation">
    Complete the initial raid stages to earn Spirit Tokens. You receive approximately 15 tokens per successful clear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Totem Investment">
    Save 75 Spirit Tokens to purchase a Raid Totem. This item is expensive but provides a significant damage boost that allows for faster clears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Team Composition">
    Ensure your team has a mix of high-DPS units (like Gala or Megumi) and slow/stun supports to keep mobs within your kill zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Boss Phase">
    Focus all fire on the boss while ensuring no small mobs enter its 10% HP-steal radius. This is the most common point of failure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Optimization Issues">
Large waves of enemies in raids can cause significant lag on lower-end PCs. If your frame rate drops, place your units further back to ensure they have more time to process attacks.
</Callout>
