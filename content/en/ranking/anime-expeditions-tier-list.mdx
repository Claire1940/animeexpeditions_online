<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Trait Rankings and Optimization">

Traits are the final layer of power in Anime Expeditions. A B-Tier unit with a God-Tier trait can often outperform an S-Tier unit with no trait. When rolling for traits, you must match the trait's bonus to the unit's specific kit (e.g., DOT traits for Sabo, Crit traits for Kenpachi).

**Best Traits for Meta Units:**
- **Unbound (0.1%)**: The ultimate damage boost. Best on **Kenpachi** and **Megumi**.
- **Primordial (0.2%)**: Massive stat increase. Ideal for units with 3+ placements.
- **Draconic (0.4%)**: Specifically boosts Damage Over Time. **Must-have** for Yuta and Sabo.
- **Money Traits**: Only for Ramen Guy to boost wave income.
