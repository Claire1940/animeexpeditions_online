### Buffing Mechanics Explained
The power of the **Hero** and **Forbidden Teacher** lies in their linear scaling. Each upgrade adds a flat 5% damage increase to all units within their range. When fully upgraded, these units can effectively double the damage output of your S-tier DPS units. 

- **Physical Focus**: Best paired with Kenpachi or Ichigo.
- **Magic Focus**: Best paired with Megumi or Freerin.
