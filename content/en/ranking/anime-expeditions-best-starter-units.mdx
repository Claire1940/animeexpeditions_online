Understanding traits is the foundation of building a high-performing squad. The game features four primary traits that can completely alter a unit's viability. Unbound is the rarest and most powerful trait, offering massive raw damage increases that make it perfect for solo-placement DPS units. Primordial focuses on SPA and range boosts, making it the ideal choice for slow-attacking utility units. Forsaken enhances critical hit capabilities, while Draconic is tailored specifically for units that rely on Damage-Over-Time (DOT) mechanics like burn and bleed.

| Trait | Rarity | Primary Buffs | Best Unit Matches |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unbound** | Mythic | Maximum Damage Output | Toy Maker, Pure DPS |
| **Primordial** | Legendary | SPA & Range Boost | Salmon Sorcerer, Slow Supports |
| **Forsaken** | Epic | Critical Hit Rate | Ichigo, Fury |
| **Draconic** | Rare | DOT (Bleed/Burn) Boost | Yuta, Ace, Gabimaru |

<Callout type="tip" title="Trait Optimization">
Always prioritize rolling Unbound for pure DPS units, while keeping Primordial for slow-attacking support units like Salmon Sorcerer.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Support Unit Rankings and Farm Meta">

Support units are the backbone of any successful run in Anime Expeditions. While DPS units deal the final blow, supports slow down enemies, freeze threats, or multiply your active damage. Additionally, generating currency is essential, making farm units like Senku indispensable.

Senku stands out as the premier epic farm unit, outperforming even some legendary alternatives in standard game modes. For high-cost game modes like raids, players often run both Senku and the legendary farm unit to sustain the expensive upgrade costs of S-tier placements. Meanwhile, crowd control units like Salmon Sorcerer provide massive utility by slowing down enemies by up to 85%, far outclassing basic options like Ice Mage or Rukia. Rukia offers a freeze mechanic, but her limited range makes her highly situational.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Starter & Progression Units (A Tier)">

A-tier units are highly usable throughout mid-game content and can even hold their own in early endgame raids. This tier features excellent synergy mechanics, particularly involving Burn and Bleed damage over time.

Ichigo stands out as a premier physical DPS unit in this tier. Although his base damage stat is lower than some peers, his kit is built around critical hits. When equipped with critical hit gear and the Forsaken trait, Ichigo achieves near-guaranteed crits on all placements. Furthermore, his Mirage Step ability summons a clone that inherits his stats and can also land critical hits, doubling his effective damage output.

| Unit | Element/Type | Special Ability | Key Synergy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ichigo** | Physical | Mirage Step (Summons Clone) | Critical Gear / Forsaken Trait |
| **Doflamingo** | Physical | Enemy Stun & Follow-up Attacks | Works well with high-DPS units |
| **Choso** | Bleed | Marks enemies for explosions | Draconic Trait / AoE setups |
| **Sabo** | Fire | Creates firewalls for extra burn | Pairs perfectly with Gabimaru |
| **Gabimaru** | Fire | Boosts DOT on burning targets | Sabo or other burn activators |

<Callout type="info" title="Synergy Spotlight">
Pairing Gabimaru and Sabo creates a devastating burn synergy. Sabo applies constant burns, which directly increases Gabimaru's DOT damage.
</Callout>

For players looking for utility, Doflamingo offers excellent crowd control. He stuns enemies in his range, triggering follow-up attacks from your other deployed units. This makes him a hybrid support-DPS unit that amplifies your entire team's damage. Choso is another powerhouse, applying constant bleed effects and marking enemies for explosive AoE damage.
