<Section icon={Swords} title="Summoning System & Banner Strategy">

Understanding the banner rotation is critical for efficient resource management. Unlike many other tower defense games, this game features a multi-tiered summoning system designed to help players target specific units more effectively.

| Banner Type | Refresh Rate | Special Mechanics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Banner** | 1 Hour | Classic rotation with three featured units |
| **Mini Banner** | 10-15 Minutes | Single featured unit/refreshes rapidly |
| **Beginner Banner** | Permanent | Guaranteed Mythic in 5 multi-summons |

The **Mini Banner** is particularly useful if you are hunting for a specific Epic or Legendary unit to fill a niche in your team. If the Standard Banner doesn't have what you need, wait for the rapid refreshes of the Mini Banner. Additionally, the **Unit Index** provides rewards in the form of gems and trait rerolls whenever you discover new units, enemies, or relics, so always check your collection progress.

<Callout type="tip" title="Showcase Feature">
Before spending gems on a banner, use the **Showcase** area in the summon lobby. You can place any unit you own to test their attack speed and AoE without starting a story mission.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unit Upgrades: Stats, Traits, & Ascension">

Once you have your core team, you must focus on three primary upgrade paths: **Stat Potential**, **Trait Rerolling**, and **Ascension**. Stat Potential determines the raw power of your unit's Damage, Range, and Cooldown. The ranking system goes from D up to **Z (Godly)**, with S and SS serving as high-tier benchmarks.

| Stat Rank | Performance Level | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Z Rank** | 100% Potential | Lock immediately; do not reroll |
| **SS Rank** | 90-95% Potential | Excellent for late-game content |
| **S Rank** | 80-89% Potential | Solid baseline for Mythic units |

**Traits** provide unique passive buffs that can completely change a unit's role. There are four Mythic traits available at launch:

| Trait Name | Primary Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unique/Monarch** | 1 Placement Limit / Massive Buffs | High-cost DPS carries |
| **Primordial** | Divine Damage Multipliers | All-around power |
| **Crit Trait** | Increased Critical Hit Rate/Damage | Fast-attacking units |
| **DOT Trait** | Damage over Time / Reduced Cost | Support or hybrid units |

<Callout type="warning" title="Ascension Warning">
Ascension is the duplicate mechanic. You need identical copies of a unit to increase their damage buffs. Only Mythic units currently benefit significantly from this system, so do not sell your duplicate Mythics.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Evolution & Progression Systems">

Evolving units in this **anime expeditions beginner guide** is handled differently than in most games. Instead of grinding specific stages for hours to find rare drops, you can utilize **Unit Quests**. When you obtain a unit like Elf Mage (Frieren), you can enter a **Unit Trial**. This is a preset challenge where you must win using a specific team. Victory grants you the evolution item immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Base Unit">
    Pull the unit from any active banner or event.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access Unit Quest">
    Open your inventory, select the unit, and click "Enter Unit Trial."
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Trial">
    Beat the stage using the provided preset team to earn the unique Evo Item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Evolution">
    Use the item in the Evolve section to unlock the unit's full passive potential.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Evolved units also gain an additional **Equipment Slot**. Base units have two accessory/equipment slots, but evolution unlocks a third slot specifically for **Unique Equipment**. This equipment is farmed through **Mastery Stages** in the Story mode.

| Equipment Type | Source | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Accessories** | Gold Shop / Drops | Basic stat boosts (Hats, etc.) |
| **Unit Equipment** | Story Mastery Stages | Second Passive / Unique Buffs |
| **Skins/Cosmetics** | Events / Shop | Visual changes and AoE colors |

<Callout type="info" title="Mastery Pity">
Mastery Stages have a 5% drop rate for unique equipment, but they feature a **20% pity system**, ensuring you get the item within a reasonable number of runs.
</Callout>

</Section>

Periodically, a specific element (e.g., Dark, Fire, Water) will receive a significant buff across all non-competitive game modes. During this time, using units of that element will grant you extra event coins and easier stage clears.
