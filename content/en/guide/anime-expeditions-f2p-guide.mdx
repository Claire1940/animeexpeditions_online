<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best F2P Traits and Evolution Priorities">

Traits provide massive permanent passive bonuses to your units, but rolling them requires rare Trait Crystals. As a F2P player, you must be extremely conservative with your rerolls. Never waste Trait Crystals on Epic or Rare units that will eventually be replaced. Save them for your S-tier Mythic and Secret carries.

Similarly, unit evolution completely transforms a character's capabilities, unlocking stronger attack patterns and higher base stats. Prioritize evolving your primary damage dealer before investing in support units.

| Trait Name | Roll Rate | Core Passive Bonuses | F2P Reroll Advice |

| **Primordial** | Mythic-tier | **+35% Damage**, -15% SPA, +20% Range | Excellent stopping point for all DPS units. |
| **Forsaken** | Mythic-tier | **+35% Crit Chance**, +35% Crit Damage, +10% Range | Perfect for fast-attacking multi-hit units. |
| **Draconic** | Mythic-tier | **+20% Damage**, +50% DoT Damage, -10% Cost | Keep on units with burn or bleed mechanics. |
