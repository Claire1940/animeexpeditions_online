Following a strict **anime expeditions upgrade order** prevents the common mistake of over-investing in units that fall off in the late game. Use the following steps to maximize your account's efficiency from day one.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Beginner Path">
    Talk to True Saint Holy in the lobby. Complete all five pages of tasks to earn Beginner Tickets. This guarantees at least two to three Mythic units within your first five multi-summons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Your Carry">
    Once you pull a top-tier DPS (Diane, Freeren, or Utah), lock them in your inventory. All subsequent gold, gems, and XP items should be funneled into this single unit until they reach their level cap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Unit Trials">
    Don't grind standard stages for evolution materials. Enter the **Unit Trial** for your specific Mythic. These trials provide a preset team, making it a test of skill rather than raw power, and guarantee the necessary evolution item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Stat Potential">
    Head to the Upgrade Section. Use your reroll tokens to aim for at least an **S-Rank** in Damage. The worthiness bar will overfill, so don't worry about "wasting" luck—it carries over to your next roll.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Stat Potential and Trait Tiers">

A critical part of the **anime expeditions upgrade order** is knowing when to stop rolling for stats. The game uses a letter-grade system that significantly impacts unit performance. While **Z-rank** is the ultimate goal, it is extremely rare.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Z-Rank (Godly)">
    The highest possible stat tier. Provides the maximum multiplier for Damage, Range, or Cooldown.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SS/S-Rank">
    The "Sweet Spot" for most players. These stats are sufficient for clearing all 2026 Raid content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="A/B-Rank">
    Temporary stats. Only keep these on secondary support units until you have enough resources to reroll.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

| Trait Name | Rarity | Primary Effect | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unique/Monarch** | Mythic | Limits placement to 1 but grants massive buffs | Main DPS carries |
| **Primordial** | Mythic | Significant raw damage increase | Any high-damage unit |
| **Crit Trait** | Mythic | Increases critical hit chance and damage | Fast-attacking units |
| **DOT/Cost** | Mythic | Decreases placement cost and adds burn/poison | Support or early-game units |
| **Money Trait** | Legendary | Increases gold earned per wave | Economy/Farm units |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Never use your last few trait rerolls on a unit that already has a functional Legendary trait. Wait until you have a stack of 50+ rerolls to hunt for Mythic traits like **Primordial**.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Evolution and Equipment Strategy">

The final stage of the **anime expeditions upgrade order** involves evolution and the mastery system. Evolving a unit doesn't just increase base stats; it changes their passives and expands their utility through equipment slots.

Evolution unlocks a third equipment slot that is exclusive to that character. Furthermore, beating the **Mastery Stage** for a map grants a 5% chance (with a 20% pity) to drop a unit's unique equipment. This acts as a second passive, fundamentally changing how characters like Kenpachi or Ichigo function in battle.

| Slot Type | Unlock Requirement | Best Equipment Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slot 1** | Default | Damage Boosters / Range Increase |
| **Slot 2** | Default | Attack Speed / Cooldown Reduction |
| **Specialized** | Post-Evolution | Unit-Specific Mastery Gear |

<Callout type="info" title="Mastery Mechanics">
Mastery equipment is essentially this game's version of a "Memoria," but it is earned through gameplay rather than gacha. Always farm the Mastery stage of a map once your main team is evolved.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Events and Competitive Progression">

To maintain your **anime expeditions upgrade order** efficiency, you must participate in time-limited events. These provide the "crowns" and "manga skins" that distinguish top-tier accounts.

- **Elemental Weather**: Pay attention to the active element. During "Dark" weather, all Dark-element units receive a massive buff across all non-competitive modes. Use this time to clear difficult Story or Mastery stages.
- **Creator Spotlight**: Play the featured creator's stage three times daily to earn unique shop currency.
- **Tournaments**: Solo tournaments are often "traitless," meaning your raw strategy matters more than your luck with rerolls. Duo tournaments allow you to carry (or be carried by) a friend.

| Event Type | Frequency | Primary Reward |
