<Section icon={Target} title="Expedition Mode: Base Building Strategy">

Expedition Mode introduces a "Clash of Clans" style management system. Your success in defending the payload depends heavily on the level of your buildings and the resources you farm.

### Essential Buildings

1.  **Gold Mine**: Farms gold using fuel obtained from missions.
2.  **Training Grounds**: Allows units to level up passively while you are offline.
3.  **Resource Drill**: Provides materials for building upgrades.
4.  **Armory/Forgery**: The hub for rerolling equipment stats.

| Building | Priority | Main Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gold Mine** | High | Infinite Yen for unit upgrades |
| **Training Grounds** | High | Maximize unit levels without grinding |
| **Armory** | Medium | Critical for endgame stat min-maxing |
| **Resource Drill** | Medium | Necessary for long-term progression |

<Callout type="success" title="Gold Farming Efficiency">
Using 6 fuel in the Gold Mine can yield up to 150,000 Gold per hour. This is the most efficient way to fund your guild and unit investments.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Master Love Quest Walkthrough">

To obtain the **Cursed Student (Manga)** skin, you must complete the Master Your True Love quest in the event stages. This requires a specific team composition and precise placement.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Team Composition">
    Form a team of four. One player must run **8th Sword (Kenpachi)**, while the other three run **Cursed Student (Yuta)**. All units must be evolved and max level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Early Placement">
    Place the **8th Sword** in the center of the path. Prioritize upgrading him to level 9 or 10 to unlock his full AOE (Area of Effect) capabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mimicry Setup">
    Scatter the **Cursed Student** units around the 8th Sword. Their passive, **Curse Mimicry**, will trigger follow-up AOE attacks based on the 8th Sword's hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utility Support">
    Ensure at least one player brings a slow (e.g., **Rukia**) and another brings a physical buff (e.g., **The Hero**). Place slows at the very edge of the range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Boss Execution">
    Defend the payload until the boss spawns (approx. 150k-250k HP). Use all modifiers for Yen to ensure max upgrades before the boss reaches the payload.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Payload Health">
The boss will likely damage your payload. Do not panic if health drops; as long as the boss dies before the payload reaches zero, you win the quest.
</Callout>

</Section>
