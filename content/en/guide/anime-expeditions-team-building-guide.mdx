<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Synergy, Traits, and Weather Systems">

Once you have established a basic team, you can optimize your performance by diving into advanced systems: Stat Potential, Traits, and Elemental Weather. These mechanics separate casual players from those who dominate the leaderboards.

### Stat Potential and Traits
Every unit has a Stat Potential that can be rerolled using worthiness points. Stats scale up to **Z-grade** (the equivalent of Godly), followed by SS and S. Additionally, rolling powerful Mythic Traits on your units can completely change their playstyle.

| Trait Name | Rarity | Tier | Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Unbound** | Mythic | S-Tier | Massive damage buff, limits placement to one unit |
| **Primordial** | Mythic | S-Tier | High percentage damage increase across all stats |
| **Crit Trait** | Mythic | A-Tier | Increases critical strike chance and multiplier |
| **DOT Trait** | Mythic | A-Tier | Adds damage-over-time and reduces overall placement cost |

### The Elemental Weather System
One of the most unique aspects of the game is the Elemental Weather system. Periodically, a specific element (such as Dark or Fire) receives a massive damage buff across all non-competitive game modes. 

When building your team, always check the active weather. If Dark units are currently buffed, swapping out an unbuffed Mythic unit for a buffed Epic Dark unit can often result in higher overall team DPS.

<Callout type="info" title="Forge Fever Event">
Keep an eye out for the Forge Fever lobby event. Evolving your units during this event doubles the stat increases they receive, making it the perfect time to upgrade your main DPS.
</Callout>
