<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Evolution and Trait Reroll Systems">

The core of end-game power in Anime Expeditions lies in rolling high-tier traits and evolving your characters. The game features an integrated auto-reroll system that allows you to hold down the roll button for rapid trait cycling, which is significantly faster than using external auto-clickers. Traits provide massive passive multipliers that can completely transform a unit's viability.

| Trait | Rarity | Primary Effect | Best Equipped On |
|---|---|---|---|
| Unbound | 0.1% | Massive overall damage and speed boost | Main DPS units like Uta |
| Primordial | 0.2% | High base stat scaling and damage multipliers | Hybrid damage dealers |
| Forsaken | Legendary | Increases critical hit damage and critical chance | Fast-attacking multi-hit units |
| Investor | 0.2% | Boosts gold and resource acquisition rates | Farm units like Speed Wagon |
| Precision True | Rare | Enhances range and overall attack accuracy | Support and utility units |

In addition to traits, units have individual stats ranging from standard letter grades up to SSS and the coveted Z-tier. You can reroll these stats using gold, and you also have the ability to transfer high stats from bench units to your primary team. Transferring a stat costs 10,000 gold per transfer. Be careful when locking stats during rerolls; locking more than one stat slot drastically increases the gold cost per roll, making it highly inefficient for non-whale players.

Evolution elevates your units to their absolute peak potential. Evolving a unit like Uta requires a Student ID, Spirit Blue, Spirit Purple, and Uta's exclusive equipment (Uta's Ring), which can be farmed from specialized Legend stages. 

| Unit | Required Items | Material Source | Evolved Passive Upgrade |
|---|---|---|---|
| Uta | Student ID, Spirits, Ring | Unit Trial, Legend Stages | Follow-up attack deals 100% plus 40% damage |
| Ger | Upgrade Crests, Spirits | Challenges, Crafting | Applies mark making enemies take 20% more damage |
| Choso | Spirit Blue, Blood Vial | Legend Stages, Crafting | Enhances bleed application and stack speed |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Game Modes and Progression Walkthrough">

To acquire the materials needed for character progression, you must navigate a variety of specialized game modes. Story Mode serves as your primary source of experience points and starter gear. Once you clear the basic stages, you must transition to Legend Stages and Challenges to farm evolution spirits and exclusive equipment.

Challenges rotate every 30 minutes, and you are limited to 10 challenge completions per day. This makes it critical to log in regularly to check for specific spirit drops. For high-end progression, the Daily Challenge (such as King's Tomb) offers the best rewards, though it features extreme difficulty scaling. Enemies in these modes possess heavy shields and massive health pools, with normal enemies scaling up to 90,000 HP and bosses exceeding 118,000 HP.

| Game Mode | Level Requirement | Key Rewards | Entry Limit |
